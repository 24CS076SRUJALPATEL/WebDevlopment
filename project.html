ABOUT THIS PROJECT :-

    WHAT IS TRAFFIC LIGHT ?​

  • A traffic light is a signaling device, usually found at intersections and pedestrian crossings, that uses red, yellow, and green lights to regulate the flow of traffic. The red light indicates that vehicles must stop, the yellow light means they should prepare to stop, and the green light allows them to proceed​

    • PURPOSE:-​

  • Traffic lights are designed to coordinate the movement of vehicles and pedestrians, preventing collisions and promoting smooth traffic flow. ​

    • CONTROL WAYS:-​

  • Traffic lights can be controlled manually, by timers, or by sophisticated computer systems that adjust the timing of the lights based on traffic volume and other factors. ​ ​

    Here we are going to make traffic control light circuit in Tinkercad software which can be controlled manually by selecting input switch A and B. ​

    • COMPONENT USED IN CIRCUIT:-​

  • In tinkercad software we are going to use NOT gate, AND gate and OR gate , Wires, some Resistors, 2 slideswitch, ​ Battery and three LED light Red, Green and Yell ​

    • WORKING OF CIRCUIT:-​

  • We design a circuit of traffic light simulator in tinkercad which can be operated or controlled manually.​ First, select two input slideswitch A and B and Now we can blink LED manually with the help of gates.​ Second, for Green light which indicates allow to procced vehicles. At 0 0 input of A and B ,connect input to NOT gate so output of NOT gate will be 1 and then input NOT gate’s output to AND gate and output will be 1. so, Green LED will be blink.​ ​ ​

  • For Red light which indicates vehicles must stop. At 1 1 input of A and B ,connect input to AND gate and output will be 1. so, Red LED will be blink.
  • For Yellow light which means they should prepare to stop. At 0 1 or 1 0 input of A and B ,connect input 0 to NOT gate so output of NOT gate will be 1 and connect input 1 to AND gate and connect output of NOT gate which is 1 to AND gate so, output will be 1. so, Yellow LED will be blink.
